About

Meet Lynn

Lynn Germain makes beaded jewelry by hand, one piece at a time. What began as a quiet pastime has become a small collection of necklaces, earrings, and bracelets — each one threaded, counted, and finished at her own table.

She works in seed beads and stones, favouring deep teals, warm coppers, and corals. Because every piece is made by hand, no two are quite the same. If you find one you love, it is almost certainly the only one of its kind.

— Lynn Germain
